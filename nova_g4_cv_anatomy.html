A
Education
Most recent first. Include GPA only if it strengthens your application — typically above 15/20 on a Portuguese scale.
Include: degree, institution, dates, GPA if strong
B
Experience bullets
Show what you did, the scale or context, and a result. Avoid vague phrases like "assisted with" or "supported the team."
Avoid: "Helped with analysis and presentations"
C
Skills
Keep it factual and brief. Don't list skills you couldn't be tested on in an interview.
Include: languages with level, software you use regularly
D
Length and format
One page for students and recent graduates. No photos, no graphics, no colour unless you are in a creative field.
