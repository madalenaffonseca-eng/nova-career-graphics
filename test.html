Typical consulting application process
Step 1 Online application CV + cover letter. Deadlines are strict — apply early.
Step 2 Online tests Numerical, verbal, and situational judgement.
Step 3 Case interview 1–2 rounds. Structured problem-solving under time pressure.
Step 4 Partner interview Final case + fit questions with a senior member.
Timeline
Large firms recruit October–December for the following September start.
Case prep
Start preparing at least 6–8 weeks before your first interview.
Internship route
Most large firms convert a significant share of summer interns to full-time offers.
