Investment banking — typical recruitment timeline
Sep–Oct
Applications open
Most bulge-bracket and boutique firms open graduate applications. Deadlines fill fast — do not wait until October.
Deadline risk
Oct–Nov
Online tests & HireVue
Numerical reasoning, situational judgement, and video interviews run in parallel with applications.
Prepare in advance
Nov–Jan
Assessment centres
Group exercises, case studies, and competency interviews. Usually one day at the firm's offices.
Practice cases
Jan–Mar
Offers issued
Graduate offers for the following September. Internship offers come earlier — typically December to February.
Ongoing
Jun–Aug
Summer internships
10-week programmes. Most large banks convert the majority of interns to full-time graduate offers.
Conversion route
